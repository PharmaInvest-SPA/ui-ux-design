# 🚀 Workflow Objectifs Management Platform

## 📌 Overview

A modern web-based workflow management application designed to help organizations define, distribute, and monitor monthly objectives across their hierarchical teams.

The platform enables supervisors to assign objectives to their delegates and operators, track yearly performance distribution, and manage customer hierarchy through a centralized administration interface.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

The main goal of this application is to simplify:

* Monthly objective planning
* Team performance tracking
* Hierarchical management
* Customer organization
* Business workflow monitoring

The application provides a structured environment where supervisors can manage objectives and follow their team's progress throughout the year.

---

# 🏗️ Application Structure

The platform is organized around four main views:

---

# 📊 View 1 — Monthly Objectives Entry

## Purpose

Allows supervisors to create and assign monthly objectives to their team members.

## Features:

* Create monthly objectives
* Assign objectives to delegates
* Assign objectives to operators
* Define target values
* Manage objective distribution
* Validate submitted objectives

---

# 📅 View 2 — Annual Objectives Distribution

## Purpose

Provides a global visualization of objective allocation throughout the year.

## Features:

* Yearly objective planning
* Monthly distribution tracking
* Performance overview
* Objective comparison
* Progress monitoring

---

# 👥 View 3 — Hierarchy Management

## Purpose

Helps organize the company's operational structure.

## Features:

* Manage supervisors
* Manage delegates
* Manage operators
* Create organizational hierarchy
* Assign users to their responsibilities

Example hierarchy:

```
Supervisor
    |
    ├── Delegate 1
    │       |
    │       └── Operator 1
    |
    └── Delegate 2
            |
            └── Operator 2
```

---

# 🏢 View 4 — Customer Management

## Purpose

Organizes and manages new customers inside the business structure.

## Features:

* Add new customers
* Organize customer hierarchy
* Manage customer information
* Associate customers with teams
* Improve customer tracking
